Demo 1: Basic Image Comparison

Below are some examples high-resolution and standard-resolution images so you can compare their quality side-by-side. Images with a green border are the high-resolution, and images with the red border are the standard-resolution. The image browser widths are set at 320 pixels.

The high-resolution image widths will be 320 pixels multiplied by the device pixel resolution, so a device with a pixel resolution of 2 would request an image width of 640 pixels. However, a standard monitor with a pixel ratio of 1 or a device with a slow connection would still only request an image that's 320 pixels wide.
